2. Resolution of Data Quality Note ⚠️


A key finding throughout the analysis was the covariate imbalance in the previously_watched_channel feature (P=0.0275).
* Imbalance Details: The Control Group had a higher proportion of previous watchers (30.0% vs. 27.9% in Treatment). This group typically clicks more.
* Resolution: Advanced modeling (Logistic Regression) was used to control for this variable. The adjusted Odds Ratio (1.4128) remained highly significant (P=0.000), confirming that the treatment effect is robust and not an artifact of the failed randomization check.

The analysis provides STRONG EVIDENCE for immediate implementation.


Recommendation Details:
